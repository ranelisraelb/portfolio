My first big break.

Grid Consulting is a real estate consulting company who's purpose is to help real estate agents reach their goals. I remember having imposter syndrome when first being contracted by Grid, but I'm incredibly thankful for the company believing in me and giving me solid footing in starting my career as a web producer.

From web design to digital marketing, I've gained practical skills in and outside of my field that I've been able to prove useful for other client. Apart from building their WordPress website, I'd say my biggest accomplishment I've had with them was taking the lead on producing and marketing their housing market podcast; None Size Fits All.

I made tailored marketing material for different media platforms and social media outlets, gained useful experience in Google Analytics and developed a knack for project management.
