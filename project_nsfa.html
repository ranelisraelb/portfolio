None Size Fits All

This venture holds a special place in my heart, the amount of experience I received ranging from graphic design and video production to digital marketing and project management was my biggest take away from this project.

I worked closely under the co-founder of the company to plan, produce and deploy 22 episodes across 2 seasons with different production styles, following strict deadlines and theme outlines. This project took a lot of back and forth between him and I and Asana was our sword and shield. We also aimed to create an efficient and effective workflow that would decrease how long it took to get from recording to deployment. By the end, we probably were able to shave a off a couple of hours and were running like a well oiled machine.

Some programs I used for this project were Cockos Reaper Audio Production, Adobe After Effects, Premiere, Illustrator and Photoshop. Apart from that, I also gained experience with soft skills including tailored social media coordination to different platforms and media types and data analytics.
