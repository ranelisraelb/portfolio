Hi, my name is Ranel and I love to make web content.

From start to finish, the process of jotting down a thought into my phone's note app to sharing with my mom the "final draft" of that note materialized into a polished product is a process that I don't mind losing sleep over. Whether it's a digital invitation for my neice's birthday or an entire website redesign for a new client, hearing "Wow, this looks great!" makes it all worthwhile.

In August of 2018, I completed a certification in Front-End Development from the University of Washington and did my best to only move up from where I was the day before. By March of 2019, I've managed to land 6 clients ranging from a real estate consulting start up to a cultural non-profit orginization where I've met amazing people, made meaningful strides, and gained valuable skills in and outside of my field.

When my laptop is closed however, I do enjoy partaking in the outdoors, jamming out at home to my favorite tracks, or grabbing drinks with friends and family. I'm really into cars, the stock market and looking for new oppurtunies to learn.
